    Jarvis

    I was envious of Tony Stark and decided I could get part way there by building an Alexa Skill, which is what Jarvis is. I'm adding new interactions all the time, so check it out! At the moment, I'm focusing on electrical and mechanical lookups and calculations.

    Set up an Amazon Echo device and add the "Jarvis" skill. Say "Alexa ask Jarvis help" to find out more.
